How to Play

60-second crash course — you'll be fragging in no time.

WASDMove. Strafe-jump (hold Space) to build speed.
SpaceJump. Time it on landing to bunny-hop (bhop) and fly.
ShiftCrouch. Slide if you're moving fast.
LMBShoot. RMB scopes the sniper.
RReload. 1/2/Q swap weapons.
EClass ability — Blink, Dash, Cloak and more.
TabHold to see the live scoreboard.
EscPause. First to 30 kills wins the match.
Pro tip: headshots hurt more, killstreaks earn glory, and daily challenges in your Profile pay bonus XP.
