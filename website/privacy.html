Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

This page explains what information ILCARTIGO collects about you, how that information is used, who else sees it, and the rights you have over it. We try to keep this short and plain.

1. Who we are

ILCARTIGO is a free browser-based arena shooter operated as a personal project. The site is reachable at ilcartigo.com. The operator can be contacted at tiberiumatei1978@gmail.com.

2. What we collect

We collect the minimum necessary to operate the site and the game:

  • Game preferences. Your chosen class, weapon, map, FOV, mouse sensitivity, and crosshair settings are stored locally in your browser via localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Connection data. When you click "Play Online," your browser opens a WebSocket connection to our game server. The server sees your IP address (for the duration of the session), the inputs you send (movement keys, fire events, ability triggers), and your position in the game world. We do not log or store this data after the session ends.
  • Analytics. If we enable analytics in the future, this section will be updated and consent will be requested through the cookie banner before any analytics scripts run.
  • Advertising cookies. The site may display ads served by Google AdSense. AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to deliver ads. See section 4 below for details and opt-out instructions.

3. What we do not collect

  • We do not require accounts or sign-ups.
  • We do not collect your real name, email, or payment information.
  • We do not sell personal data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not use third-party tracking beyond what AdSense provides (when ads are enabled) and what the cookie banner discloses.

4. Google AdSense & third-party cookies

If we enable advertising on the site, Google AdSense and its partners may store cookies on your device to deliver and measure ads. These cookies are governed by Google's privacy policy, not ours. You can:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.
  • Visit policies.google.com/technologies/ads for full details on how AdSense uses cookies.
  • Use the cookie banner on this site to reject non-essential cookies. Your choice is remembered in localStorage.

5. How long we keep data

Game session data (positions, inputs, hit events) lives only in server memory during your active connection and is discarded when you disconnect. Local preferences live in your browser until you clear them. We do not operate a database of player history at this time.

6. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we hold about you (currently: none beyond an active game session).
  • Delete your local preferences by clearing your browser's site data.
  • Object to processing of your data — primarily by rejecting cookies on the banner or opting out of personalized ads.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we've violated your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email tiberiumatei1978@gmail.com.

7. Children

ILCARTIGO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the site, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

If we materially change what data is collected or how it's used, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change and, for material changes, we'll display a notice on the homepage for at least 30 days.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: tiberiumatei1978@gmail.com.
